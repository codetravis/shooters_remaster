Transfers

We welcome any gun transfer to or from a valid FFL holder. Our transfer fee is $25 to send or receive.

We are a listed FFL dealer on GunBroker.com.

It is our policy that we will not accept transfers from individuals as we have had issues with receiving from individuals in the past.

Multi-gun Transfer Fees

For multiple guns received from or sent to an FFL dealer in the same shipped box, $25 for the first gun and $10 for each additional gun. AR-15 lowers are considered a gun and the same transfer fees apply.

Receiving a Transfer

Transfer fees and shipping charges of firearms from the originating FFL dealer we receive are the responsibility of the buyer in addition to our $25 transfer fee.

Sending a Transfer

A transfer fee of $25 plus shipping charges will be required before a firearm is sent. Any transfer fees charged by the FFL dealer receiving a firearm are the responsibility of the person receiving the transfer.

Transferring from us or to a valid FFL dealer

If you would like to use us to transfer a firearm to or receive from a valid FFL holder for you, we will need the following information from you:

  • Your name and contact information (address and phone number - email address is optional)
  • FFL Dealer transferring from:

    Name, address, phone, fax, and a contact name.

    We will contact the FFL dealer to confirm shipping and FFL information.
