Gun Rentals

A variety of handguns and long guns are available for range rental. Handguns are $10 and long guns $15 each to rent plus ammunition.

We require our range ammunition to be purchased and used for all rental guns.

Range ammunition prices start at $16.95 per box of 50 and go up from there based on caliber.
